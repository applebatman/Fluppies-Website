ABOUT US

Fluppies began in July 2002 and although it was initially inspired by Pugsley (the loveable Pug featured all over this site), the range has been extended to include all dog breeds, shapes and sizes. Fluppies is based in Australia, but can deliver products to you all over the world. At Fluppies, you will find that every dog imaginable with every need imaginable, is catered for. Our canine friends are our very best, and we dress them accordingly. Our products are the highest quality in both fabrics and materials, and craftmanship. Most of the dog coats, clothing, dog hats, collars and dog boots are hand-made, so each piece is unique in itself. Our prices are competitive and reasonable, as are our freight and handling costs. So please, take a look at our products. We know you will be impressed with the range and the quality.
